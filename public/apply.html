Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Yes. All information is encrypted using bank-level 256-bit SSL encryption. We never share your personal data with anyone without your consent.

Will this impact my credit score?

No. We only perform a soft credit inquiry during the pre-qualification process, which does not affect your credit score in any way.

What types of businesses can you fund?

We work with businesses across all industries including construction, restaurants, retail, trucking, medical, and more. If your business has been operating for at least 6 months, we likely have an option for you.

What happens after I submit the application?

A dedicated funding specialist will call you within 5 minutes to discuss your options. From there, you'll receive a personalized quote with rates, terms, and funding amounts.

How fast can I get funded?

Most businesses receive funding within 24-48 hours of approval. Some same-day options are available depending on your qualifications.
