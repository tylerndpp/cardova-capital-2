Common Questions
Frequently Asked Questions

Will applying affect my credit score?

No. Our initial pre-qualification uses a soft credit check that does not affect your credit score.

How fast can I receive funding?

Most businesses receive funding within 24-48 hours of approval. Some merchant cash advances can be funded same-day.

What are the minimum requirements?

At least 3 months in business, a business bank account, and minimum monthly revenue of $10,000.

How much capital can I qualify for?

Funding amounts range from $5,000 to $5,000,000 depending on your business revenue and financial health.

Is there a cost to apply?

Absolutely not. Applying is completely free with no obligation.
